Final Project - Developing Data Projects

This project consists in creating and deploying a Shiny App to showcase the student's ability in developing a data project.

My choice was to create a simple app that includes R functions with the reactive capabilities of Shiny.

The app prompts you to choose songs so that you can elect your favorite song.
